# 🌍 Airbnb Global Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze **Airbnb global performance data** and extract meaningful business insights.

The focus of this project is not just visualization, but **analytical thinking, data modeling, and decision-making logic** behind the insights.

It simulates real-world business intelligence scenarios where data is used to support **strategic decisions, pricing optimization, and performance evaluation**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze global Airbnb performance trends  
- Understand user behavior and booking patterns  
- Evaluate pricing strategies across regions  
- Identify seasonal demand variations  
- Measure trust and performance metrics  
- Support data-driven business decision-making  

---

- Interactive filters and slicers  
- Dynamic KPI tracking  
- Regional performance breakdown  
- Trend visualization over time

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Clear identification of high-performing regions and listings  
- Strong seasonal patterns affecting demand  
- Pricing directly influences occupancy and revenue  
- A small percentage of listings contribute to majority of performance (Pareto effect)  
- Trust and reviews play a key role in customer behavior
